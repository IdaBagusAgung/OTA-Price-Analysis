AIRBNB BALI DATA DICTIONARY
==================================================

room_id:
    Unique identifier for the Airbnb property

check_in_date:
    Guest check-in date (YYYY-MM-DD format)

check_out_date:
    Guest check-out date (YYYY-MM-DD format)

stay_duration:
    Length of stay in days

locale:
    Language/country locale setting (e.g., en-US)

currency:
    Currency used for pricing (e.g., USD)

check_in_year:
    Year of check-in

check_in_month:
    Month of check-in (1-12)

check_in_day_of_week:
    Day of week for check-in (0=Monday, 6=Sunday)

check_in_week_of_year:
    Week number of the year for check-in

full_url:
    Complete Airbnb URL for the booking

data_extraction_date:
    Date when data was processed

analysis_version:
    Version of the analysis
